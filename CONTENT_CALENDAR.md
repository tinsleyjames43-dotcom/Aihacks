# 8-Week Strategic Content Roadmap

## Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1-2)
### Week 1
- Deliverables: Conduct a survey for target audience feedback.
- Content Categories: Blog Posts, Social Media.
- Success Metrics: 100 survey responses, 20 social media shares.
- Maintenance Notes: Analyze survey results for key insights.

### Week 2
- Deliverables: Compile survey results and create a summary report.
- Content Categories: Report, Video Summary.
- Success Metrics: 1 report, 500 video views.
- Maintenance Notes: Prepare the report for presentation.

## Phase 2: Development (Weeks 3-4)
### Week 3
- Deliverables: Draft key content pieces based on feedback.
- Content Categories: Blog Posts, E-books.
- Success Metrics: 3 draft posts, 1 e-book.
- Maintenance Notes: Review drafts and incorporate feedback.

### Week 4
- Deliverables: Finalize content and design assets.
- Content Categories: Infographics, Emails.
- Success Metrics: 5 infographics, 3 email campaigns.
- Maintenance Notes: Test email campaigns for functionality.

## Phase 3: Launch (Weeks 5-6)
### Week 5
- Deliverables: Launch website and content.
- Content Categories: Website, Social Media.
- Success Metrics: 1000 website visits, 50 shares.
- Maintenance Notes: Monitor website traffic and engagement.

### Week 6
- Deliverables: Host a live launch event.
- Content Categories: Webinar, Blog.
- Success Metrics: 200 live attendees, 100 recordings viewed.
- Maintenance Notes: Gather feedback from attendees.

## Phase 4: Evaluation and Optimization (Weeks 7-8)
### Week 7
- Deliverables: Collect data on user engagement and feedback.
- Content Categories: Analytics Report, User Interviews.
- Success Metrics: 300 engagement interactions, 10 interviews.
- Maintenance Notes: Prepare report based on findings.

### Week 8
- Deliverables: Optimize content based on gathered data.
- Content Categories: Updated Blog Posts, FAQs.
- Success Metrics: 20 updated posts, 50 FAQ views.
- Maintenance Notes: Implement changes and prepare for next phases.
